Our Courses

Advanced -General Woodwork (5 days) R5 850 / student (6 students per class)
You will learn:

  • Learn to use free software to design your own side table.
  • Master the principles of efficient wood use with cutting programs.
  • Understand the essentials of various measuring tools.
  • Gain practical knowledge of a wide array of woodworking hand tools.
  • Acquire hands-on experience with diverse power tools and safety measures.
  • Recognize the unique functions of various saws like the mitre saw and table saw.
  • Learn to join wood using techniques like using a Biscuit Joiner, Festool Domino or a dowel jig.
  • Discover how to laminate wood for enhancing structural elements like table legs.
  • Get practical experience using a thickness planer and a jointer planer.
  • Learn to sand wood by hand and machine for a smooth finish.
  • Discover the process of wood staining and finishing.
  • Learn about various woodworking materials and their specific applications.
  • Learn to estimate project costs using a free Google sheet template.
  • Use a variety of tools and joining techniques to build your own solid wood side table.
  • Learn how to maintain professional interactions with customers.
  • Learn to use a router and apply its techniques to your projects.
  • Immerse yourself in the full process of building a side table, from raw planks to a finished product.
  • Bonus: You will get free furniture SketchUp designs to help you get started on your first DIY or business project (worth more than R1000).

Prerequisite: You need to have basic computer skills and be very comfortable with using a computer.

Intermediate: Cupboard Design, Build & Install (5 days) R5 850 / student (6 students per class)
You will learn:

  • Understand the basic structure of a cupboard and its various components.
  • Master various measuring tools, crucial for accurate woodworking.
  • Drill precise pilot holes with a cordless drill.
  • Assemble a cupboard by screwing together melamine panels.
  • Edge a Formica top for a professional finish.
  • Trim the edging with a router for a clean, professional look.
  • Fit plastic adjustable legs to ensure stability.
  • Level cupboards using a spirit level and a laser level.
  • Fit a kickplate to protect your cupboard from everyday wear and tear.
  • Install a drawer to add functional storage space.
  • Fit and adjust a door for smooth operation.
  • Attach handles to add the finishing touch to your cupboard.
  • Accurately measure a space for your project.
  • Design your project using free software.
  • Create a comprehensive cutting and hardware list.
  • Optimize board usage and calculate project costs using free software.
  • Create a professional quotation for your project.
  • Build and install cupboards securely and correctly.
  • Understand the different materials and hardware options available for your project.
  • Gain practical experience using various types of saws.
  • Interact professionally with customers.
  • Bonus: Make a cutting board! And you will get a free kitchen and cupboard SketchUp design file to help you get started on your first DIY or business project (worth more than R1000).

Prerequisite: You need to have basic computer skills and be very comfortable with using a computer.
